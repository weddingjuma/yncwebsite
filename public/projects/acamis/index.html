ACAMIS

Challenge/ Problem

Academy of Culinary Arts is a hospitality college based in Nepal. They wanted to up their game by being able to efficiently manage their daily routines such as student enquiries, registration, attendance, assessments, etc.

Our Approach

We started with an initial study on how they go about their task currently. We documented the findings and presented them designs that had similarities with their current workflow and at the same time that made use of technology that made their life more efficient and more effective.
